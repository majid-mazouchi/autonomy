A reference companion to the whole diagnosis series. The posts use a lot of jargon — *cohort*, *DTC*, *confounder*, *dominant stressor*, *residual*, *RUL* — and this page defines all of it in one plain sentence each, grouped by theme so you can read a section or look one term up.

It covers both halves of the field: **diagnostics** (explaining a failure that already happened) and **prognostics** (forecasting one that hasn't yet), plus the statistics, causal reasoning, reliability math, quality engineering, and data/AI machinery that the series leans on.
